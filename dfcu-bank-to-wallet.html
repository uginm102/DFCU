Transfer Money from Bank to Wallet

The most convenient way to transfer from your dfcu account to a mobile wallet is via the Quick Banking app. Follow these steps to complete your transfer.

1

Log in to Quick Banking

Open the Quick Banking app and log in using your Touch ID, Face ID, or password.

2

Tap on Payments

From the home screen, tap the Payments button.

3

Tap on Transfers tab

Select the Transfers tab within the Payments section.

4

Select Mobile Money

Choose Mobile Money as your transfer method.

5

Enter transaction details

Fill in the transaction details in the form provided. Tap Continue to proceed.

6

Review & Continue

Review the payee details carefully, then tap Continue.

7

Trust Factor Authorization

Complete Trust Factor Authorization to finalize and complete the transaction.

Mobile Money transfers are processed instantly. The recipient will receive an SMS confirmation once the transfer is complete.
